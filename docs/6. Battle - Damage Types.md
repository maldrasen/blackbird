Damaging attacks all have damage types, but status effects should also be typed as well. If blind is a fire type effect then fire resistance gives a higher change to avoid the effect. 

### Physical Damage Types
Most armor will have various resistances to the three physical types. 
- *crush* 
- *pierce* 
- *slash* 

### Magic Damage Types
- *fire* - It's fire, but also provides resistance to `blind` and `burn` and some other effects.
- *shock* - Lightning damage, but some status effects like `stunned` might be lightning
- *arcane* - A more generalized magic resistance for effects like `slow` that don't really fall into another type. 
- *psychic* - Most mental effects do psychic damage. Very important resistance as a lot of status effects are mental.
- *corruption* - Demon type enemies will do additional corruption damage, which should be difficult to get resistance from. `Curse`, `fear`, and `hex` are corruption type status effects.
- *nature* - For poison and disease and such.
