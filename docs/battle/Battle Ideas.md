### Ravishing
The ability for monsters and characters to fuck during a battle is definitely something I want to add. I have a few ideas about how that would work,  but it's something I'm still thinking about.

When two characters are having sex they can occupy the same position. The characters aren't removed from the battle, but their actions are entirely sex focused until they're broken apart somehow. 

A minotaur makes a grab attack on a character in the party. That character is removed from the formation, and the minotaur's position holds both him and the character. The held character can try and break free on their turn, opposed str or dex check. The minotaur would first strip the character out of their armor, at least their pants or helmet. The character has another opportunity to break free before the minotaur starts fucking them. Other party members can attack the minotaur while this is happening, the minotaur can't defend. Enough damage will make him drop the party member. The minotaur could also his turn to move to the back rank to shield him somewhat from attacks. 

With a system like this it's unlikely that the party member would actually get raped unless the player was allowing it to happen. There could be strategic advantages to doing so though. Maybe a fight against three minotaurs would be difficult, but if you had two characters in your party that act as 'rape bait' the minotaurs would occupy themselves with the two, while the rest of the party focuses their attacks on the third. The rape bait characters could be wearing slutty bondage gear to tempt their targets, could even have an 'entice' command. This is essentially a crowd control role. As they're raped by different monsters it effects their training stats of course.

Other monsters might have charm spells that work in a similar way. Instead of breaking free from a hold they'd need to make resistance roles to break free of the compulsion. A caster might summon a 'tentacle hell' portal below a character, making that position a 'sex position' until the portal is dispelled.

The party would have the same options for capturing monsters, grapple attacks, charm spells, etc. And the monsters would have the same options to break free. A succubus monster could use these skills against the player, charming them into using their grapple attacks against the succubus so that they start raping the demons. As the succubus gets 'raped' she drains the health of the character doing it. You'd potentially need to attack your own character to stop them, or have some other way to dispel the compulsion. 

##### Solid parts of this system
- I think combining positions makes sense from a tactile stand point, as two (or more) people fucking should take up a single area on the tactile map. A spell attack on that square would hit both people.
- We should definitely make grappling attacks a thing, both for monsters and players.
- I like the 'rape bait' role. The game doesn't really have 'classes' in the traditional sense, so we'll need skills, armor, and equipment to make it clear that using a party member as bait is a viable combat strategy. We should have monsters that have that role as well, seducing and crowd controlling your party members.
- We can have this play into the capture system as well. If you've grabbed a monster, disarmed, stripped, and tied them up, you've captured them, and can decide what to do with them at the end of the battle.
