
##### Hide
A stealthy character can spend a round trying to hide. When they're hidden they can't be attacked by monsters until they make an attack or use another ability. They can heal or buff themselves though and stay hidden. An effect that targets another character should cancel the state. They can be hit by AoE type effects too. When hidden they can make a sneak attack that targets a monster in the first two ranks. Sneak attack bypasses the monster's active defenses, and hits for extra damage. 

##### Riposte
Riposte is a passive ability, though it still needs to be leaned. Learning the ability requires a fairly high parry skill. The ability gives a chance for a counter attack on a successful parry. The riposte chance depends on parry skill, so if the ability is leaned when parry skill is at 50, we can band the riposte chance like so `[50:20%, 60:40%, 70:60%, 80:80%, 90:100%]` When the counter attack is made we still roll for the attack. Only a single attack should be possible though. (No multiple swings on riposte) Monsters may also riposte, which could lead to a Disgaea like counter-counter-counter situation. Each riposte takes a successful parry of the roll though, so counters can't be infinite.
