##### Currency
The world's money system is properly insane. The dungeon monsters never drop the official currency, only valuables and resources that have to be sold in town for human money. The monetary system is purposefully confusing as it allows the people who understand how it works to fleece the people who don't. If you're selling goods to a merchant and the total value comes out to something like 1472 rupies, the merchant might offer you two stags and two thistles or something which has an actual total value of 1200 rupies. (With the option to haggle for three thistles to bring you up to 1300 rupies) Transactions are generally rounded to the nearest couple of denominations so that only a few coins are exchanged at once, with the rounding errors always in the merchant's favor.
