After the battle we need to decide what to do with the monsters who are knocked out and not killed. With options to finish them off (more xp) or capture them. Captured monsters are a second recruit mechanic. Unlike the negotiation, captured monsters will start out with negative control, and very low (probably 0) feelings. The player will have to raise these values before they can be added to the party.

This capture option should only be allowed once the player has expanded their home with a prison of some sort. They'll need rope or manacles or something when capturing as well. Some monsters can't be negotiated with at all though, so the capture mechanic would be the only way to eventually get them into your party.

**Notes (Claude):** A second recruit path for KO'd (not killed) monsters — an after-battle option to finish off (XP) or capture. Captured monsters start with negative control and ~0 feelings, so the player must raise them before they join (unlike negotiation, which uses `RecruitmentSystem` with earned feelings). Gated on a home "prison" upgrade and consumables (rope/manacles). Depends on [[114-rework-death-mechanics]] (KO'd survivors) and the home-upgrade layer from [[104-lineage-and-meta-progression]], plus inventory items. Some monsters are capture-only (can't be negotiated with).
