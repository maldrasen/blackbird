The after training enlighten view will share a similar look and feel as the after battle enlighten view, though it will have different options. We still show the skill increases, but the focus here will be allocating the anima and animus, spending them to increase the sensitivities or sexual preferences.

Like the level up view we can also unlock aspects or special abilities when their requirements are met, though only the sexual ones. There may be some cross over between combat and sexual abilities though, a seduction ability for instance. So rather than a type, abilities may need a list of tags.
